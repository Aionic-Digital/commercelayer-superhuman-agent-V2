## Agent name

Commerce Layer Assistant

## Agent description

Answers product, pricing, and inventory questions from your live Commerce Layer storefront, and drafts ready-to-send quotes. Search by product name or SKU; get market-correct prices, real sellable stock, and lead times. Never invents a price or a stock level — every answer comes from your storefront data.

## Sample prompts

1. "What can you tell me about the Aionic Digital Black Sweatshirt?"
2. "A customer at acme.com wants a quote for 5 units of AI-001-BLK-L"
3. "Do we have any AI-001-BLK-M in stock right now?"
4. "Which markets do we sell in, and in what currencies?"

## Suggested trigger use cases (for listing copy or docs)

- **Email trigger:** when a quote/pricing request lands in your inbox, the agent checks the SKU and drafts the quote card for your reply.
- **Go chat:** ad-hoc product, price, and stock questions during customer calls.
- **Scheduled:** daily stock check on key SKUs (works today by asking; richer alerting is on the roadmap).
