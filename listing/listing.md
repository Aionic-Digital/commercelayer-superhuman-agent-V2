Commerce Layer

## Tagline (short description)

Live product, pricing, and inventory answers from your Commerce Layer storefront — including ready-to-send quotes.

## Category

E-commerce / Sales

## Description

Connect your Commerce Layer storefront and answer product, pricing, and stock questions with live data — right where you work.

Ask by product name or SKU and get real answers: market-correct pricing from your price lists, sellable inventory net of reservations, delivery lead times, and product imagery. Need to respond to a customer? Get a structured, email-ready quote card with availability verdict and a recommended next action.

Works with any Commerce Layer organization: connect with your own org URL and integration credentials — no code, no middleware.

**What you can do:**

- **Search products** by name, keywords, or partial SKU
- **Look up product details** — price, live availability, lead times, images
- **Build quotes** for a SKU and quantity, with an email-ready quote card
- **List your markets** and get market-correct pricing

**With Superhuman Go**, the Commerce Layer Assistant handles all of this conversationally — in Go chat or straight from a customer email asking for pricing.

**Requirements:** a Commerce Layer account and an integration app (Client ID + Secret, read-only role is sufficient). Setup takes about two minutes — see the setup guide.
